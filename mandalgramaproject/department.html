Mandal Departments

Administration

Officer: Mandal Officer

The administrative head for the Kuppam Revenue Division is the Sub-Collector and Sub-Divisional Magistrate/Revenue Divisional Officer (RDO), Sri S. Sivaiah or Sri K. Srinivasulu Raju (sources vary, indicating a recent or pending transition). The Kuppam mandal office, or Tehsildar office, operates under this division. The municipality is overseen by Commissioner V. Srinivasa Rao.
